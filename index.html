Luca Cirfeta

Senior Software Engineer & Team Lead — event-driven backend systems by day, gravitational-wave signal analysis on the side.

Based in Rome, Italy

About

Senior Software Engineer and Team Lead with 5+ years of experience in enterprise backend development, leading a team of 7 developers with end-to-end ownership: architectural design, Agile delivery, CI/CD, infrastructure security, and direct client engagement. I design microservices in Java 17/21 with Spring Boot following hexagonal architecture and SOLID principles, and build event-driven integrations with Apache Kafka. Outside of that, I spend part of my time applying machine learning to gravitational-wave data — pulling faint, unclassified signals out of detector noise, which is not so different from the day job.
